Wild Youth

Wild Youth is designed and shared by Jeremy Vessey. Wild Youth Font is a neat and feminine handwritten script font, created manually using brush pen. Wild Youth Font is perfect for: Logos, branding, apparel, scrap booking, wedding invitations & more.

The Global Typeface

Global is a serif typeface with multilingual applications. Slender lines and great legibility make it perfect as a display font. With curves in all the right places, it is great for editorials and other commercial use. Global Typefamily consists of 772 characters.
